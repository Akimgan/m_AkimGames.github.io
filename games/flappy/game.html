Rules

The object of the game is to control the flight of the bird, which moves continuously between the rows of green pipes. Upon collision with them, the game ends. Control is made by buttons by touching the screen or clicking with the left mouse, in which the bird makes a small jerk upward. If there is no jerk, the bird falls due to gravity, and the game also ends. Points are earned for each successful flight between two tubes. The gameplay does not change throughout the entire game.
