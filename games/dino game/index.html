DinoRunGame!

Rules

In game "DinoRunGame" you will need to click to make the jump. The dinosaur will run and along the way there will be cacti that you will have to jump over, and on the right you will see a score - the number of cacti you jumped over.After losing, click on "ok" to continue the game. Try to run as long as possible!
